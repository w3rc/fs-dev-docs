Clusters are instances of deployment for ComfyUI or APIs based on ComfyUI. They manage the execution of your workflows across multiple containers.


## Key Features
  - Scalability: Clusters can scale horizontally by adding more containers.
  - Load Balancing: Distribute workload across containers for optimal performance.
  - Deployment Management: Manage deployments of workflows and APIs.

## How to Use Clusters

Whenever you start a ComfyUI instance or Deploy their APIs, a new cluster is created

## Benefits
  - Efficient management of large-scale deployments.
  - Improved performance through load balancing.
  - Simplified scaling of applications.
